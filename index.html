Omisagwe (Groundnut Soup) Recip

Omisagwe is a very delicious soup that is popular among the wanno people of Edo state. It is usually served with swallow of choice.

Recipe Information:

  • Time to prepare: 45 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 3 people to 5 people
  • Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients for the soup

  • 1 cup of grounded groundnut
  • 3 to 5 peices of beef
  • 120ml of palm oil
  • 1 tablespoon of grayfish
  • 1 cup of washed bitterleaf
  • small onions choped
  • 3 to 4 pieces of yellow paper
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • And of course, water!

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season beef with seasoning cube, onion, some pepper and salt and steam in its juice till dry. Add water and cook till almost well done.
  2. Add more water(about 50 cl)to the pot together with crayfish. Allow it to boil
  3. Now, Add the remaining pepper and onion leftover from boiling the beef
  4. Pour the cup on grounded groundnut in a bowl, add water to turn it to a paste
  5. Now, Pour the paste into the pot of soup and mix with your cooking spoon
  6. Allow the soup to Cook for about 20 minutes
  7. Add the washed bitter leaves and cook for another 3 minutes
  8. Drop from fire. Serve with swallow of choice or rice

Tips

  1. Do not maake the mistake of pouring your grounded groundnut to the pot directly to avoid having lumps
  2. It can also be used for eating massa
